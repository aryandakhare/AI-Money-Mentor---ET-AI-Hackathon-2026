## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

**India's Investment Ecosystem:**
- **Demat Accounts:** 14+ Crore (140M+)
- **Active Retail Investors:** 5 Crore (50M)
- **Passive Savers (no investment):** 50+ Crore (500M)
- **Average Household Income:** ₹10-15 Lakh/year

**TAM Calculation:**
```
TAM = Households with investable income × Average annual spend on financial advice
    = 10 Cr households × ₹5,000/year avg
    = ₹50,000 Crore/year addressable market
```

**Target Segment:**
- Age: 25-55 years
- Income: ₹10L-1Cr/year
- Digital literacy: Smartphone + internet
- Financial awareness: Beginner to intermediate

**SAM Calculation:**
```
SAM = 3 Cr households (serviceable segment) × ₹5,000/year

```

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) - Year 1

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risk
**Risk:** Low adoption (< 1 Lakh users by EOY)
**Mitigation:**
- Partner with ET for distribution
- In-app promotions in ET Prime
- Referral incentives (₹100 per friend)
- Target early adopters (existing ET Market users)

### Regulatory Risk
**Risk:** RBI/SEBI scrutiny on investment advice
**Mitigation:**
- Clear disclaimers: "AI-assisted, not financial advice"
- No direct transaction execution
- Recommendation audit trail
- Compliance review by legal team

### Technology Risk
**Risk:** Claude API changes, increased costs
**Mitigation:**
- Multi-model approach (fallback to cheaper models)
- Cache recommendations (reduce API calls)
- Hybrid model (rules-based + AI)
- Target Claude cost: < ₹0.5 per user/month

### Churn Risk
**Risk:** High churn after first month
**Mitigation:**
- Free Money Health Score + FIRE plan (high-value)
- Reminders for life events (new baby, raise, promotion)
- Tax season campaigns (Jan-Feb, Jul-Aug)
- Community features (share goals with friends)
